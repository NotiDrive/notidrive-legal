NotiDrive – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 December 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how NotiDrive (“we”, “us”, “our”, or the “App”) collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using NotiDrive, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account details – such as your email address and name when you sign up or log in.
  • In-app preferences – such as settings, reminders, and notifications you configure.
  • Support communications – such as messages you send to us via email or in-app support.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data – such as how you interact with the App, which features you use, and basic technical information (device model, OS version, app version).
  • Diagnostic data – such as crash reports or performance data, to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

2.3 Location Information

With your permission, NotiDrive uses your location to detect when you are approaching or entering certain charge zones (for example, congestion or low-emission zones) so we can send relevant alerts and reminders.

  • Location may be used in the background to support driving alerts.
  • You can disable location access in your device settings at any time, but some core features may stop working.

We do not use precise location for advertising targeting. It is used only to provide the App’s driving and notification features.

2.4 Subscription & Payment Information

Premium subscriptions are managed by Apple’s App Store or Google Play. NotiDrive does not store your full payment card details. We may receive limited information from Apple/Google (for example, subscription status, country, and whether you are in a trial or an active period).

2.5 Advertising and Analytics

The free version of NotiDrive may show ads using third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA or Android Advertising ID).
  • Approximate location (e.g. general region, not precise GPS).
  • Basic device and usage information.

This information is used to show relevant ads, measure ad performance, and help keep the free version of the App available.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide core App functionality (alerts, reminders, zone detection).
  • Maintain and improve the App’s performance and reliability.
  • Personalise certain aspects of your experience (e.g. settings).
  • Communicate with you about updates, changes, or support issues.
  • Show ads in the free version (where applicable).
  • Meet legal or regulatory requirements, if necessary.

4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Where data protection laws apply (for example, in the UK or EU), we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide you with the App and related services.
  • Legitimate interests – such as improving the App and preventing abuse or fraud.
  • Consent – for things like optional analytics, ads personalisation, or notifications where required.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers – such as hosting providers, analytics tools, and ad networks that help us operate and improve the App.
  • Platform providers – such as Apple and Google, in order to process subscriptions, payments, and store purchases.
  • Authorities or law enforcement – where required to do so by law, or to protect our rights or the rights of others.

6. No Affiliation with Transport Authorities

NotiDrive is not affiliated or officially connected with Transport for London (TfL) or any other transport authority. When you follow a link to an external site (for example, a payment portal), you are subject to that site’s own terms and privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide and maintain the App,
  • Comply with legal obligations,
  • Resolve disputes, and
  • Enforce our agreements.

You can request deletion of certain information (for example, by contacting us), though some data may need to be retained for legal or technical reasons.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • Accessing the data we hold about you,
  • Requesting corrections,
  • Requesting deletion,
  • Objecting to certain types of processing, or
  • Withdrawing consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at notidriveapp@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

NotiDrive is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. International Transfers

Our service providers may be located in different countries. Where required by law, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are made means you accept the updated Policy.
